# Crafting the Perfect Resume for Internship Applications

# Crafting the Perfect Resume\n\nYour resume is often your first impression with recruiters. A well-crafted resume can open doors to internship opportunities.\n\n## Resume Fundamentals\n\nLength should be one page for undergraduates. Format should be clean and professional. Key sections include contact information, professional summary, education, experience, skills, and certifications.\n\n## Contact Information\n\nInclude full name, phone number, email address, city and state, LinkedIn URL, and GitHub profile if applicable.\n\n## Education Section\n\nInclude school name, degree, major, expected graduation date, GPA if strong, and relevant coursework.\n\n## Experience Section\n\nUse action verbs like developed, implemented, designed, analyzed, and created. Include quantifiable results when possible.\n\n## Skills Section\n\nGroup by category. List most relevant first. Be honest about proficiency.\n\n## Common Mistakes\n\nAvoid typos, inconsistent formatting, vague descriptions, and irrelevant information.\n\n## Conclusion\n\nA well-crafted resume significantly increases your chances of getting interviews.
