# Nursing Internship: Launching Your Healthcare Career

# Nursing Internship\n\nNursing internships, often called nurse residency programs, are crucial stepping stones in nursing education. These programs provide hands-on experience in patient care and clinical skills development.\n\n## Types of Nursing Internships\n\nClinical placements include medical-surgical, ICU, emergency, pediatrics, obstetrics, and psychiatry. Settings include hospitals, clinics, long-term care facilities, and home health agencies.\n\n## Essential Skills\n\nClinical skills include patient assessment, vital signs monitoring, medication administration, and wound care. Communication skills are essential for patient and team interaction.\n\n## Preparation\n\nEducational requirements include completion of nursing prerequisites. Certifications include BLS, ACLS, and PALS.\n\n## First Week Goals\n\nComplete orientation, meet the nursing team, understand unit workflows, and begin shadowing your preceptor.\n\n## Conclusion\n\nA nursing internship is a critical step in launching your nursing career.
